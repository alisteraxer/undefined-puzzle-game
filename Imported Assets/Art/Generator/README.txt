IMPORTANT:
This 3D model is designed for a low poly + pixel art style.
Textures are intentionally kept at 128×128 resolution to maintain the retro aesthetic.

Depending on your game engine, some post-import adjustments may be required to preserve the intended look:

- Disable mipmaps to avoid blurriness.

- Use nearest filtering to keep pixel edges sharp.

- Set texture compression to lossless or uncompressed, if possible.

These steps are common when working with pixel art assets in Unreal Engine, Godot, Unity, or similar tools.
